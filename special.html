Collector’s edition “Gurieli from the Country of Rustaveli” consistes of nine species of tea: Traditional Black, Traditional Green, Earl Grey, Jasmine Blossom, Alpine Berries, Wild Mint, Chamomile, Snow Rose and Meskhetian Nettle. The Georgian and English versions of the collector’s edition were presented in 2019.

Each variety of Georgian version of tea is packed with works of such famous painters as: Petre Otskheli, MikhailZichi, Giorgi Eristavi, Mikheil Chikovani, Gregory Gagarin, Boris Yohansen, Mikheil Chiaureli.As well as the fresco of Shota Rustaveli from Jvari Jerusalem monastery.
